In this episode of Abnormal DevOps Iterations, we sit down with Yasmin Rajabi, the COO of StormForge, a company specializing in Kubernetes optimization.

Yasmin shares her journey from Puppet to StormForge, discussing how her team uses machine learning to automate and optimize Kubernetes configurations.
She dives into the challenges of Kubernetes resource management, the importance of automation, and how StormForge helps organizations reduce waste and improve efficiency. Yasmin also shares her thoughts on the future of Kubernetes, AI-driven infrastructure, and the evolving role of DevOps in the enterprise.

Whether you're a Kubernetes expert or just getting started, this conversation offers valuable insights into the world of cloud-native technologies and the future of infrastructure automation.
